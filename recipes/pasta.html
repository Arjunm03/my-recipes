Pasta recipe

Description

Here is a recipe for some marinara pasta!

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • tomato paste
  • basil or oregano
  • salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to skillet
  2. Add garlic to oil
  3. Add tomato paste and crushed tomato
  4. Let it simmer for 30 minutes
  5. After simmering add basil, oregano, and salt and pepper
