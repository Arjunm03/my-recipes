Grilled Cheese recipe

Description

Here is a recipe for a nice grilled cheese!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 slice Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat
  2. Butter one side of slice of bread
  3. Place butter side down on skillet and add 1 slice of cheese
  4. Butter another slice of bread and put on cheese butter side up
  5. Grill until lightly brown and cheese is melted
