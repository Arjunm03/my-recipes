Mac and Cheese recipe

Description

Here is a recipe for some homemade mac and cheese!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cook macaroni according to package directions and drain
  3. Melt butter in medium skillet over low heat and gradually add flour whisking until well combined
  4. Slowly poor milk and whisk until smooth
  5. Stir in cheeses and cook over low heat until cheese is melted
  6. Put macaroni in dish and poor sauce over it
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes then serve
